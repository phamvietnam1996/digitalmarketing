Tools & Technologies

I will introduce the tools and technologies I use first, because we absolutely use these tools in this project, and I will show how to use it later in the details. There are many tools for data analytics, but here I just go into the details for the most important tools.

The first tool is Google Analytics. Google Analytics is a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. Google Analytics became the most widely used web analytics service on the web.

The second tool is Ahrefs. Ahrefs is a well-known paid toolset for website and SEO analysis. If you are looking for a comprehensive SEO website analysis, then Ahrefs should be your preferred choice as it provides key SEO details. It can also analyse website of your competitors.

The other digital marketing channel is Facebook advertising. Facebook ads are becoming known as one of the cheapest way to advertise online and reach many potential customers.

Description

This part will give the detailed description of the tools' features which will help us to process the data analytics.

Google Analytics offers an easy and free way to track and analyze visitors on your website. You just have to follow the instructions to add the tracking code to your website or mobile app so you can collect data in your Analytics property. Here is the 10 benefits of using Google Analytics:
• Target your online visitors.
• Evaluate traffic flow through your website.
• Know what users are searching on your website.
• Targeted Campaigns.
• Allows for Campaign Tracking.
• Customized Data/Reports.
• Goal Setting & Monitoring.
• Automated Emails.

We can use Ahrefs tool to analyse the competitors' website or any website by just searching the domain name.

For example, I analyse my current website's company SW Consulting.
